Topic3

Start with the classic temples of Anuradhapura or entertainment hubs of Colombo, but explore a little further to make your own fresh and exciting discoveries

Aacross SriLanka, you will find cultural heritage of the highest international standards. See industry-leading architecture and design, and witness a highly seasonal aesthetic in everything from picture-perfect gardens to departments stores and cuisine. Leave the towns and cities behind, and discover hot spring hamlets in the mountains, white sandy beaches and an array of adventure activities.
